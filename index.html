An update.

Given what the world has been thrown into, this is no longer the right time for a new fitness business to be getting off the ground.

So for the moment, I'm putting the launch of Space on the back burner until we're out the other side of the disruption, and the business climate becomes clearer.

Space is an experience that I'm still very passionate about being able to offer, but we'll just have to wait a little longer than planned.

The membership presale is closing for now, and a refund is on the way to all of you that had made a purchase or donation. Presale memberships will be made available again as soon as we're back in action.

I believe exercise is monumentally important for mental health, especially in a time of such uncertainty, and with many self-isolating. So with Space on pause, I'll be turning my attention to finding ways to connect people with group exercise from their homes, to try and limit the disruption that COVID-19 is causing in this part of peoples lives.
